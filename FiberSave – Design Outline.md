## **FiberSave – Design Outline** 

## **Overview** 

FiberSave is a non-custodial savings and remittance application built on CKB's Fiber Network. It enables users, particularly in emerging markets, to save in Bitcoin or RGB++ stable assets, transfer funds instantly at low cost, and participate in digital savings circles without relying on traditional financial institutions. 

## **Problem** 

Millions of people rely on informal savings groups and expensive remittance services to preserve and move value. 

Existing solutions suffer from: 

- High remittance fees 

- Slow settlement times 

- Limited access to stable savings products 

- Custodial risks 

- Lack of transparency in group savings arrangements 

There are currently no Fiber applications designed specifically for these use cases. 

## **Proposed Solution** 

FiberSave provides: 

- Personal BTC/RGB++ savings vaults 

- Instant remittance through Fiber 

- Savings goals and progress tracking 

- Group savings circles 

- Non-custodial fund ownership 

- Low-cost transfers using payment channels 

## **MVP Scope** 

## **Phase 1: Personal Savings** 

Users can: 

- Create an account/wallet 

- Deposit BTC or RGB++ assets 

- Create savings goals 

- Track progress toward goals 

- Withdraw funds at any time 

## **Phase 2: Fiber Remittance** 

Users can: 

- Send funds instantly through Fiber 

- Generate payment requests 

- View transaction history 

- Share payment links 

## **Phase 3: Group Savings** 

Users can: 

- Create savings circles 

- Invite members 

- Define contribution schedules 

- Monitor contributions 

- Track payout cycles transparently 

## **User Flow** 

## **Personal Savings** 

Onboard 

- → Connect/Create Wallet 

- → Create Savings Goal 

- → Deposit Funds 

- → Monitor Progress 

- → Withdraw Anytime 

## **Remittance** 

Connect Wallet → Enter Recipient 

- → Specify Amount 

- → Route Payment Through Fiber 

- → Recipient Receives Funds Instantly 

- → Transaction Recorded 

## **Savings Circle** 

Create Circle 

- → Invite Members 

- → Members Deposit Contributions 

- → Contributions Tracked On-Chain 

- → Funds Distributed According to Rules

## **Why This Matters** 

FiberSave demonstrates a real-world use case for Fiber beyond developer experimentation. It showcases how instant Bitcoin-native payments and CKB infrastructure can address practical financial challenges faced by millions of users globally. 

It has the potential to become both a flagship consumer application for Fiber and a candidate for further ecosystem funding.
